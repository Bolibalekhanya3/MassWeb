Vision

Massabielle High School aspires to be a shining example of excellence, dedicated to shaping learners who are God-fearing, independent, confident, and self-sufficient-ready to face the world with integrity and strength.

Mission

- We embrace all learners who have passed, regardless of their grades, religious backrounds, or home situations.
- We foster unity and cooperation within our school community
- We commit to punctuality and diligence, ensuring all tasks are completed on time during working hours.
- We uphold order, good conduct, and respect as fundamental values for everyone every day.
- We strive tirelessly to ensure every learner achieves at least 50% in every subject, unlocking their full potential.
- We actively discover and nurture the unique talents of each learner.
- We begin each day inited in prayer, strengthening our community and purpose
